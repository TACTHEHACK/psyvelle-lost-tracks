# Psyvelle Lost Tracks

This public research project is trying to locate people who remember or preserved the music released under the artist name **Psyvelle** in 2026.

The Spotify catalogue currently known to us contains **204 tracks across 21 albums**. The releases remain visible in playlists and catalogue metadata, but the tracks are no longer playable.

## Known identifiers

- Spotify artist: [Psyvelle](https://open.spotify.com/artist/5mvavjIutBHVd8G7ilINK3)
- Spotify research playlist: [all known Psyvelle tracks](https://open.spotify.com/playlist/6lwXVRPcDyYeBJDCxdoa1J)
- Copyright shown in Apple Music: **© 2026 PV Studio**
- Composer shown in Apple Music metadata: **Anthony Coleman**
- Release period in the exported catalogue: **2026-01-27 to 2026-04-23**

## The aim

The immediate goal is not to build a fan discography. We are trying to find the **missing audio** and people who may have legally purchased, downloaded, cached or otherwise preserved any of these releases before they disappeared.

One local recording is currently confirmed: **California Soul** from *THE UNDISPUTED TRUTH – The Undisputed Truth*. It is a different-sounding recording from the familiar original version.

## Can you help?

Please [open an issue](../../issues/new) if you:

- listened to Psyvelle on Spotify, Apple Music, Amazon Music or another service;
- still have one or more tracks in a local music library;
- know anything about **PV Studio**, **Anthony Coleman**, the distributor, or the releases;
- can confirm track durations, credits, ISRCs, artwork or release dates.

Please do **not** post public download links to copyrighted recordings. We can first verify material through metadata, file hashes and audio fingerprints.
