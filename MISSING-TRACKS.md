# Missing Psyvelle Audio

## What we are looking for

These releases are still visible as metadata on Spotify, but the audio is unavailable. We are looking for people who heard, purchased, downloaded for offline use, or otherwise preserved any of these releases while they were legitimately available.

**Confirmed local recording currently known to this project:**

- `California Soul` from *THE UNDISPUTED TRUTH – The Undisputed Truth*

All other tracks are currently treated as **unconfirmed / missing** until verified.

## How to help

- Open a GitHub issue and state which album or track you remember or possess.
- Include the exact track title, album title, duration, file format, source service and any visible metadata.
- Do not post public download links to copyrighted audio.
- Audio can be compared privately by checksum, duration, codec data, ISRC and acoustic fingerprint.
